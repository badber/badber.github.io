Luis Badesa

I am a 3rd-year PhD student working on stochastic economic optimization for electricity networks.

My research aims to facilitate a cost-effective integration of renewables energies. More specifically, I focus on obtaining dynamic-security conditions and implementing them as constraints into optimization problems. The challenge comes from mapping the differential-equation-driven dynamics into algebraic constraints, all while achieving computational efficiency for the stochastic optimization. You can see a summary of my research here.

My supervisors are Prof. Goran Strbac and Dr. Fei Teng, and I am part of the Control & Power Research Group at Imperial College London.
